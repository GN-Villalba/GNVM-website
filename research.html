Research

PhD Research

  • Accreditation and Student Learning in Teacher Education: Evidence from Colombia. View PhD Poster PhD working paper using linked administrative data and quasi-experimental methods to evaluate the effects of higher education quality policy on student outcomes.
  • Pathways, Mismatch and Skill Loss in Higher Education: Evidence from Middle-Income Countries. PhD project examining the mismatch between student academic ability and institutional quality, with a focus on inequality, sorting, and skill formation.

Other Working Papers

  • Sticky Floors and Gendered Labour Structures: Evidence from Paraguay.
    Working paper derived from MSc research.
    View Draft on OSF This paper examines gender wage inequality in Paraguay, focusing on household constraints, labour market segmentation, and persistent “sticky floor” dynamics.
  • Innovation and Productivity in Paraguay: Evidence from a CDM Model.
    Co-author: Selva Olmedo. Work in progress applying the Crépon–Duguet–Mairesse framework to firm-level innovation data in Paraguay.
